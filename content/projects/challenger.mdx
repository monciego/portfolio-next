This project is a website built for **ChallengerHR**, an outsourcing company that connects global businesses with skilled Filipino virtual assistants. I worked as a **Virtual Assistant (VA)** in this company and also contributed as a **developer and collaborator** in building their website.

The goal of the website is to clearly present their **services, capabilities, and value** to potential clients, especially companies from the United States across industries such as **entertainment, healthcare, homecare, and more**.


#### Project Overview  

The website is designed to help businesses understand how ChallengerHR can support their operations through **remote staffing solutions**. It highlights different types of virtual assistant services and makes it easy for clients to explore what fits their needs.

The focus is on creating a **clean, professional, and informative experience**, allowing visitors to quickly learn about the company and take action.


#### Services Highlighted  

The platform showcases different VA roles, including:

- **Operational VAs** – handling scheduling, email management, and admin tasks  
- **Marketing VAs** – managing content, social media, and campaigns  
- **Sales VAs** – supporting lead generation and client follow-ups  
- **IT & Tech VAs** – assisting with technical support and systems  
- **Healthcare VAs** – supporting medical and administrative processes  

These sections help communicate how the company provides **scalable and flexible workforce solutions**.

#### Features  

- Clear presentation of **services and capabilities**  
- Structured sections for different **VA roles and industries**  
- Professional and modern **UI design**  
- **Responsive layout** for all devices  
- Focus on **lead generation and client conversion**  

##### Built With  

- **Next.js** – Frontend framework for fast and optimized UI  
- **Tailwind CSS** – for responsive and clean UI design
