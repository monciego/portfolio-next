This project is a web-based platform built for **Dreams PH**, a licensed real estate company in the Philippines that connects **property owners, buyers, and investors** in one system.

The platform is designed to make property transactions more **accessible, transparent, and efficient**, helping users find the right property or successfully market their listings.

#### Project Overview  

Dreams PH allows users to explore a wide range of properties, including **land, houses, apartments, condominiums, and commercial spaces**. It serves as a bridge between sellers and buyers, making the process of buying, selling, or investing in real estate more seamless.

The system focuses on delivering a **simple and user-friendly experience**, allowing users to easily navigate listings and manage their property activities.

#### Features  

- **User registration** as buyer or seller  
- Sellers can **post and manage property listings**  
- Buyers can **browse and search properties**  
- Support for different property types (land, residential, commercial)  
- **Agent assistance system** for both buyers and sellers  
- Centralized platform for **property discovery and management**  
- Clean and responsive **user interface**  

#### Platform Functionality  

The system provides flexibility for users:

- Sellers can manage their own listings or assign **agents** to handle their properties  
- Buyers can explore listings and connect with sellers or agents  
- Agents act as a bridge to ensure smoother communication and transactions  

This creates a more **organized and guided real estate experience** for all users.
