### St. Joseph Cathedral Parish Management System

The **St. Joseph Cathedral Parish Management System** is a **comprehensive web-based platform** designed to streamline the scheduling of **church events**, management of **certificates**, and tracking of **donations**. This system allows both **church administrators and parishioners** to efficiently handle requests, approvals, and records with ease.

The system is built for **St. Joseph the Patriarch Cathedral Parish (Alaminos Cathedral)**, a historic Catholic church located in **Alaminos, Pangasinan, Philippines**. Dedicated to **Saint Joseph the Patriarch**, the cathedral has been the episcopal seat of the **Diocese of Alaminos since 1985**.
