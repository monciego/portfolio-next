### Streamlining Healthcare Management for PSU

The **Pangasinan State University Infirmary System** is a **web-based medical record management system** designed to enhance **patient care and administrative efficiency** within the university’s healthcare facility.

This system provides **doctors, dentists, and nurses** with a **centralized platform** to manage patient records, treatments, medications, and medical examinations. By automating critical processes, it improves **record-keeping, diagnosis tracking, and medicine inventory management**, ensuring a more efficient healthcare workflow.

---

### ✨ Key Features

- **🔑 Secure Login** – Nurses, doctors, and dentists log in with unique credentials for role-based access control.

#### 🩺 Doctor Features

- **📊 Doctor Dashboard** – Displays key analytics, including **monthly patient visits**, distribution of **medical vs. dental cases**, and **examination requests** through interactive bar graphs.
- **👩‍⚕️ Nurse Account Management** – Allows doctors to **create and manage nurse accounts**, simplifying staff onboarding.
- **🔍 Quick Patient Lookup** – Enables doctors to search for **clinical and treatment records** using patient IDs for faster access to medical history.

#### 🏥 Nurse Features

- **📌 Interactive Dashboard** – Provides an overview of **total students, teaching staff, and non-teaching staff**, ensuring seamless tracking of the institution's healthcare needs.
- **📁 Clinical & Patient Records** – Nurses can **add, edit, and manage** patient profiles, consultation records, and treatment history.
- **💊 Medicine Inventory** – Tracks available medications, **automatically updating stock levels** as medicines are dispensed.
- **📄 Report Generation** – Allows nurses to generate reports based on **school year, semester, and case type (medical/dental)** for documentation and administrative purposes.
- **📝 Examination Records** – Supports **pre-employment, annual check-ups, and OJT examinations**, with doctors assigned to handle patient evaluations.

---

### 🏗️ Built With

- **Laravel** – For backend development and efficient data handling.
- **MySQL** – Ensuring reliable and structured database management.
- **Tailwind CSS** – For a clean, responsive, and modern user interface.
- **Alpine.js** – For lightweight, interactive front-end functionality.

This system plays a **crucial role** in improving **healthcare services at PSU**, offering a structured and **automated approach to medical record management**, benefiting both medical personnel and patients.
