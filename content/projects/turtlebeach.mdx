This project is a website built for **Turtle Beach**, a beachfront resort and restaurant located in **San Juan, La Union, Philippines**. I developed this website while assisting as an **IT support** at the resort, and as part of a client project under **TechniqalGroup**, where I am a **partner and developer**.

The goal of the website is to showcase the resort’s **accommodations, amenities, nightlife, and overall experience**, helping attract tourists and provide clear information for guests.

#### Project Overview  

The website highlights what makes Turtle Beach a unique destination in La Union—from its **front beach location** to its vibrant **nightlife and entertainment scene**. It is designed to give users a complete view of the resort experience, from relaxing stays to lively events.

The layout focuses on **visual storytelling**, allowing visitors to explore the resort through images, content sections, and detailed information.

#### Features  

- Showcase of **resort amenities and beachfront experience**  
- Detailed view of **rooms**, including the Sky Suite experience  
- Sections for **nightlife, live bands, and DJ events**  
- Highlight of **entertainment events and celebrity appearances**  
- Display of **activities and guest experiences**  
- **Dining and bar menu** presentation  
- **Gallery** of photos for visual exploration  
- **FAQ and contact information** for guest inquiries  
- Fully **responsive design** for mobile and desktop users  

#### Website Content  

The platform includes multiple sections to cover the full resort experience:

- Home and overview of the resort  
- Rooms and accommodations  
- Dining and bar offerings  
- Events and entertainment  
- Activities and experiences  
- Gallery and media  
- FAQ and contact details  

#### Built With  

- **Next.js** – Modern React framework for scalable frontend development  
- **Tailwind CSS** – for modern and responsive UI
